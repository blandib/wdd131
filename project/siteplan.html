Site Name: Recipe Ledger

A step-by-step recipe guide for home cooking

Site Purpose:Recipe Ledger

This website serves as a digital cookbook, providing structured recipes with clear instructions. Users can browse, search, and follow detailed cooking guides to master various dishes.

Target Audience:

Home cooks, beginners looking for easy recipes, and experienced chefs wanting structured cooking methods.

Scenarios:

“I want a beginner-friendly guide to making pasta from scratch.”


How can I improve my baking skills with detailed instructions?


Color Schema:

Primary Color: Warm orange (#FF8C00) – Used for buttons and accents.


Secondary Color: Cream white (#FFF5E1) – Used for backgrounds to keep a clean look.


Accent Color: Dark brown (#4E342E) – Used for headings and footer.


Highlight Color: Bright Red (#E40C2B) – Used for attention-grabbing elements (alerts, key highlights).


Supporting Color: Deep Green (#438945) – Used for success indicators or earthy-themed sections.


Soft Gold (#F2D349) – Used for subtle highlights and decorative accents.


Typography

Headings: Playfair Display


Body Text: Open Sans


Emphasized Text: Roboto Slab
